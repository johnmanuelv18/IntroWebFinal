Flowers FAQ

Answers to all your flower questions.

Do you offer same-day flower delivery?

+

Yes, we offer same-day flower delivery for orders placed before 2 PM local time. Our team ensures that your fresh flowers arrive promptly, making them the perfect last-minute gift.

Can I use a discount voucher for shipping fees?

+

Yes, you can apply eligible discount vouchers to cover or reduce shipping fees. Simply enter your voucher code at checkout, and the discount will be applied to your order total, including shipping costs.

What types of occasions do you cater to?

+

We cater to a wide range of occasions including birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, holidays, and more. Our floral arrangements are thoughtfully designed to suit any celebration or sentiment.

How can I track my flower delivery?

+

After placing your order, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use this link to monitor your delivery in real-time and ensure your flowers arrive on time.
