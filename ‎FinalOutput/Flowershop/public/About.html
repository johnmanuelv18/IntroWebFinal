About Us

Our Story

Flori began as a simple idea, born from a deep love for the beauty and emotion that flowers can convey. It all started in a small, cozy greenhouse on the outskirts of town. Our founder, Amelia Flori, grew up surrounded by vibrant gardens cultivated by her grandmother. From a young age, she was captivated by the myriad of colors, shapes, and scents that filled the air. Each flower told a story, evoking memories of joyful celebrations, heartfelt apologies, and love blossoming in its many forms. This passion for flowers was nurtured throughout her life, and after years of working in the corporate world, Amelia decided it was time to follow her true calling.

With little more than her savings, a truckload of enthusiasm, and an unwavering belief in her dream, Amelia opened the first Flori flower shop. It was a small, quaint space, but it quickly became known for its exquisite arrangements and personal touch. Word of mouth spread, and soon, Flori was the go-to florist for weddings, anniversaries, and every special occasion in between. What started as a humble shop has now grown into a thriving business with multiple locations, a dedicated team of talented florists, and a loyal customer base that spans generations. At Flori, we believe that every bouquet we create is a work of art, crafted with love and attention to detail, because we know that flowers have the power to speak the language of the heart.

Delivering Freshness

At Flori, we understand that freshness is key to creating beautiful, long-lasting arrangements. That's why we go the extra mile to ensure that every flower in our shop is at its peak. We work closely with local growers to source the freshest flowers, often picking them the same day they're delivered to our shops.

We Take Sustainability Seriously

At Flori, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's a way of life. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact and promoting eco-friendly practices in every aspect of our business. From sourcing flowers from sustainable farms to using recyclable and compostable packaging.

Supporting Local Products

We believe that supporting local businesses is crucial to the health and vitality of our community. That's why, at Flori, we're committed to sourcing as many of our flowers and materials as possible from local growers and artisans. By doing so, we're able to offer our customers the freshest, highest-quality products while also supporting the local economy.

Our Extended Journey

As Flori continued to grow, so did our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community. We realized early on that success wasn't just about selling flowers; it was about making a positive impact on the lives of our customers, our employees, and the environment. Over the years, we've expanded our offerings, from luxurious flower arrangements to thoughtful gifts and unique home décor items, all while maintaining our unwavering dedication to excellence. Our team of skilled artisans works tirelessly to ensure that every product we offer is crafted with the highest standards of quality.

Our journey has also led us to build strong relationships with local growers and suppliers. By sourcing our flowers and materials locally whenever possible, we're able to reduce our carbon footprint, support small businesses, and offer the freshest blooms available. We've also introduced eco-friendly practices in our operations, from using sustainable packaging to implementing energy-efficient processes in our shops. At Flori, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us from the beginning. That's why we're proud to partner with local charities and organizations, donating flowers, time, and resources to causes that matter to us and our customers. Our extended journey is about more than just growth—it's about staying true to our values and continuing to bring joy to people's lives, one flower at a time.

The Blooms Nature Intended

At Flori Flower Shop, we believe in the pure beauty of nature. That's why our arrangements are crafted using only the freshest.
