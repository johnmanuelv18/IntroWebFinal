Flowers FAQ

Answers to your questions about our flowers and services.

Can you send flowers to someone special?

+

Absolutely! Flori specializes in delivering beautiful, fresh flowers to your loved ones, no matter where they are. We offer a wide range of arrangements perfect for any occasion, including birthdays, anniversaries, or just to brighten someone's day. Our delivery service ensures your flowers arrive fresh and on time, anywhere in the country.

Are flower deliveries a good gift idea?

+

Yes! There’s nothing quite like receiving a bouquet of fresh flowers. They make the perfect gift for any occasion—whether it’s a celebration, an apology, or just because. With Flori, you can choose from a variety of stunning arrangements to fit any sentiment.

What occasions are flowers suitable for?

+

Flowers are suitable for all occasions. From weddings and birthdays to sympathy and thank you arrangements, flowers are a universal symbol of love, care, and affection. At Flori, we have something for every occasion.

How can I place an order for flower delivery?

+

Ordering from Flori is easy. Simply browse our selection online, choose your preferred arrangement, and select the delivery option at checkout. Our team will handle the rest, ensuring your flowers are hand-delivered with care.
